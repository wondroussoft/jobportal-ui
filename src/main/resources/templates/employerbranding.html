FAQs

  • What kind of technology do I need to run a Virtual Career Fair?

    vFairs runs on the cloud so you or your participants don’t need to install software to access the online Career Fair. All you need is a standard web browser and a stable internet connection.

  • How long can a Virtual Career Fair be?

    We can run Virtual Career Fairs as long as you want, however, we have observed that events that last around 2 days work the best in terms of ROI. It also depends on the nature of the Career Fair.

  • How much time does it take to setup a Virtual Career Fair?

    A typical Virtual Career Fair that has around 10 Virtual Booths can take 1-2 weeks to set up. This includes configuring branding elements, designing booths, uploading content and scheduling webinars. Customizations to the default features, however, can take more time and are estimated on a case-to-case basis.

  • What kind of support and training do you offer?

    As soon as you sign up, we assign you a dedicated event manager who takes care of your needs before, during and after the event. Our technical team remains on standby to resolve any issues while your Virtual Career Fair is in progress.
